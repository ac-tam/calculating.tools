FAQ
How do I use this Calculator?
Copy and paste the entire table from PupilPath

How do I assign weights to my weighted classes such as AP or IB classes?
Open the menu by clicking the box above. Where it says "Keyword", enter a phrase that identifies the class in PupilPath such as "AP" or "PHYSICAL EDUCATION". In Weights, enter the weight. For example, Unweighted classes are 1.0, AP classes are 1.1, and Gym is 0.5. In credits, enter how many credits the class grants. You may have to check your transcript to find this, but most are 1.0 per semester.

Do I need to enter data for every class in the "Edit Weighted Classes"
No. If you don't enter any data, it defaults to a weight of 1 and a credit value of 1.

The Average says NaN? What happened?
Check to make sure you copied the entire table. If you captured half of a row, then the calculator won't work.

The average is different from my transcript average
Allow a 0.1 variance since pupilpath rounds their course averages. In addition, some teachers round grades up or down which is not relefected in PupilPath.
